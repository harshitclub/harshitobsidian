## Logical Thinking
1. If you have 3 switches outside a room and 1 bulb inside, how will you identify which switch controls the bulb (you can enter the room only once)?
2. A number is doubled and then 10 is added. The result is 50. What was the original number?
3. If you rearrange the letters “CIFAIPC”, what meaningful word can you form?
4. Why is it faster to search in a sorted list than an unsorted list?
